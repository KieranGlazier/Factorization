\title{Leaf Bomb}

Wrapping 8 leaves of the same type around a ball of \#{item.factorization:sap} sap
creates a Leaf Bomb.
Throwing the Leaf Bomb against a solid surface will sprout a burst of organic leaves;
a true pearl in the horticulturist's toolbox.

Fortune can be applied to the leaf bomb in an \#{tile.anvil.intact}anvil, creating larger and larger bursts of leaves as the fortune level increases.
(Whether or not this is actually worthwhile is debatable; you will usually get more leaves than you put in, even with no enchantment.)

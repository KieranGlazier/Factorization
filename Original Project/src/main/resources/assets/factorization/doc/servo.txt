\title{Servos}

The servo is a mobile \link{socket}{socket}.
Any tool that can be equipped on a socket
can be equipped on a servo.

Servos ride on \link{servorail}{servo rail}.
They move along this rail in a particular direction,
and its socket can point perpendicular to the direction of motion.

Servos need \link{charge}{charge} to run.

They are controlled by \link{servodecor}{instructions}.

They can be \link{servocolor}{colored}.

In addition to the socket part, servos can hold a single stack of items.
